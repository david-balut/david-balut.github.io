##### Abstract

We use transmission electron energy-loss spectroscopy (T-EELS) with energy resolution ΔE ≈ 30 meV and momentum resolution Δq ≈ 0.01 Å⁻¹ to study the charge excitations in Bi₂Sr₂CaCu₂O₈₊ₓ. We find a highly damped plasmon whose linewidth is comparable to its energy at low momenta (q < 0.15 Å⁻¹), which does not disperse but instead evolves into an incoherent continuum at larger momenta. We conclude that Bi-2212 behaves as an incoherent metal with strongly damped charge excitations, and find no evidence for previously reported RPA-like plasmon behavior.
